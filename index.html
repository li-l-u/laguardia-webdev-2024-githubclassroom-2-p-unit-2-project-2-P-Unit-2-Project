Historic Landmarks

Italy's preservation of historic monuments is well known. Like the giant colosseum that was built almost 2000 years ago.

Explore and Enjoy the Local Arts

On the streets, you will see vendors selling and showcasing several forms of their art. Consider buying one of their one-of-a-kind works for a memorable souvenir.

In the wonderful city of Florence, you can also explore the expansive wineyards and grape farms, trying your hand at wine tasting (if you are of legal age haha).

And of Course, Eat the Food!

Cuisine is the highlight of most trips, and Italy is definitly not lacking in this department either!
